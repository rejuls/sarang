About Sarang Memorial

Sarang memorial flood light tournament is an annual competition held in memory of Sarang, a second year mechanical student (2014-18) who had an untimely farewell due to an accident.

He was a football enthusiast who had an immense passion for the game. The event serves as a memory of him. Tournament boast about 25 or more teams every year vying for the prestigious cup.
